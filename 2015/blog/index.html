Data on CS Graduate School Admissions

A collection of official, quantitative data on the Masters and PhD admissions process for computer science graduate schools. Several top schools are included in the study with official admission statistics with GPA and GRE scores. Every data point has a citation to the original source. Data is from 2010 to 2014. Other components of the graduate application (e.g. letters of recommendation) are not included in this study.
