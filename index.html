Projects

Swarachakra

Swarachakra Bangla is a touch-screen keyboard for inputting text in Bengali, the 7 th most spoken language by the total number of native speakers. It displays the consonants sequenced according to the logical structure of Bengali script, phonetically grouped and arranged in a grid similar to those found in most school textbooks.
